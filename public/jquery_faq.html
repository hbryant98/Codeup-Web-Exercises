National Parks FAQs

How many acres of land do the National Parks cover?
There are over 407 sites in the National Park System covering more than 84 million acres of land
How many miles long is the Appalachian trail?
The Appalachian trail is a 2,185 mile footpath that traverses scenic, wooded, wild lands
How many people visit the National Parks each year?
In 2014, more than 290 million people visited National Parks
What is the nation's deepest cave?
America's deepest cave is at Carlsbad Caverns; it is 1, 593 feet deep
What is the oldest protected area (park) in America?
Hot Springs National park, which became a park in 1921.
How many species of vertebrates are supported in Yosemite National park?
There are over 400 species of vertebrates including fish, bird, mammals, amphibians and reptiles
How many waterfalls are in Yellowstone National Park?
There are over 300 waterfalls, 1 active volcano and thousands of petrified trees
How many battlefields or military parks are in the National Park System?
There are 25.
How many seashores are in the National Park System?
There are 10 seashores.
How many National Monuments are in the National Park System?
There are 78.

Arches National Park

  • This site features more than 2,000 natural sandstone arches, including the famous Delicate Arch.
  • Devils Garden Campground is located eighteen miles from the park entrance and is open year-round.
  • Facilities include potable water, picnic tables, grills, and both pit-style and flush toilets.
  • Bring your own wood or charcoal for the grills.

Badlands National Park

  • The Badlands are a collection of buttes, pinnacles, spires, and grass prairies.
  • Extreme temperatures, lack of water, and the exposed rugged terrain led to this name.
  • To fully experience most of what the Badlands has to offer, it takes two days.
  • To drive through the park and stop at a few overlooks, it takes about two hours.

Carlsbad Caverns National Park

  • Carlsbad Caverns has 117 caves, the longest of which is over 120 miles (190 km) long.
  • The park is open every day, except Thanksgiving, Christmas (December 25) and New Year's Day (January 1), weather and road conditions permitting.
  • Purchase cave tickets at the ticket counter inside the visitor center.
  • Self-guided and ranger-led tours of Carlsbad Cavern are available daily.
