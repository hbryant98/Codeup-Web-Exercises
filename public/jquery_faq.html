National Parks FAQs

How many acres of land do the National Parks cover?
There are over 407 sites in the National Park System covering more than 84 million acres of land
How many miles long is the Appalachian trail?
The Appalachian trail is a 2,185 mile footpath that traverses scenic, wooded, wild lands
How many people visit the National Parks each year?
In 2014, more than 290 million people visited National Parks
What is the nation's deepest cave?
America's deepest cave is at Carlsbad Caverns; it is 1, 593 feet deep
What is the oldest protected area (park) in America?
Hot Springs National park, which became a park in 1921.
How many species of vertebrates are supported in Yosemite National park?
There are over 400 species of vertebrates including fish, bird, mammals, amphibians and reptiles
How many waterfalls are in Yellowstone National Park?
There are over 300 waterfalls, 1 active volcano and thousands of petrified trees
How many battlefields or military parks are in the National Park System?
There are 25.
How many seashores are in the National Park System?
There are 10 seashores.
How many National Monuments are in the National Park System?
There are 78.
